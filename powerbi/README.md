# Power BI Dashboard

This folder contains the Power BI dashboard developed for supply chain intelligence and demand forecasting.

## Dashboard Pages

### Executive Overview

- Total Demand
- Total Units Sold
- Average Inventory
- Average Discount

### Demand Analysis

- Demand by Category
- Demand by Region
- Demand by Seasonality
- Promotion Impact

### Inventory Intelligence

- Stockout Risk
- Overstock Risk
- Inventory Coverage

### Pricing Analysis

- Price vs Demand
- Competitor Pricing Impact
- Discount Performance

### Forecasting Dashboard

- Actual Demand
- Predicted Demand
- Forecast Accuracy

## Objective

Provide decision-makers with an interactive platform for monitoring supply chain performance and demand trends.
